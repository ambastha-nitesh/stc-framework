SEGMENT PERFORMANCE

Cloud Services Segment:
Revenue of $12,450 million in FY2024 represented growth of 22.1% year-over-year.
The segment benefited from strong enterprise cloud adoption, with annual recurring 
revenue (ARR) reaching $14.2 billion, up 25% from $11.4 billion in FY2023. 
Customer count exceeded 85,000, with net revenue retention rate of 125%.

Enterprise Software Segment:
Revenue of $8,320 million grew 5.5% year-over-year, driven by price increases 
and cross-selling. License revenue declined 8% while subscription revenue grew 
18%, reflecting the ongoing transition to cloud-based delivery. Segment operating 
margin was 32%, consistent with the prior year.

Professional Services Segment:
Revenue of $3,280 million increased 5.8% year-over-year. Utilization rates 
averaged 78%, up from 75% in FY2023. The segment supported 1,200 enterprise 
implementations during the fiscal year, with average project duration of 14 months.

KEY RISKS

The company faces risks including: increased competition in cloud markets, 
cybersecurity threats, regulatory changes affecting data privacy and AI, 
macroeconomic uncertainty, and the need for continued investment in research 
and development to maintain competitive positioning.
